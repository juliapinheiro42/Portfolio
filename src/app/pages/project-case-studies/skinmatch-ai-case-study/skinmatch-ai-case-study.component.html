01

Overview

SkinMatch AI is a full stack AI-assisted product prototype created to explore personalized skincare recommendations. The project was inspired by the overwhelming amount of skincare content on social media: ads, influencer reviews, product launches and recommendations that often feel generic or disconnected from each person’s real skin needs.

02

The Problem

Social media makes skincare discovery easier, but also more confusing. Users are constantly exposed to product recommendations, sponsored content and reviews, but it is difficult to know whether a product is compatible with their skin type, concerns, routine or sensitivities.

A product that works well for one person may cause irritation, acne, oiliness, dryness or no visible result for another. SkinMatch AI was created to explore how technology could reduce this uncertainty.

03

Why I Built It

I built SkinMatch AI because I personally felt this problem. I often saw skincare products being recommended online, but I did not have a clear way to understand if they made sense for my own skin. That frustration became the starting point for a product experiment focused on personalization, explainability and decision support.

04

The Solution

The solution is a guided recommendation experience where the user provides information about their skin profile, concerns and preferences. The system can then process this context and generate more personalized suggestions or analysis, helping the user understand why a product may or may not be a good fit.

Final Summary

SkinMatch AI helped me explore how full stack development, AI-assisted logic and product thinking can work together to turn overwhelming information into a clearer and more personalized decision-making experience.
